## Smart Home Challenges Unique to Eden Prairie

Many of Eden Prairie's most desirable homes were built during the building booms of the 1980s through early 2000s. These homes typically have adequate electrical service (200-amp panels) but may lack the structured wiring needed for today's smart home systems. Cat5 cabling, common in late-1990s builds, may need upgrading to Cat6 for reliable 4K video distribution and high-speed networking.

Eden Prairie's terrain includes significant elevation changes, particularly in neighborhoods near the Minnesota River bluffs south of Flying Cloud Drive. Multi-level homes built into hillsides often have connectivity challenges in walkout lower levels. Professional-grade mesh Wi-Fi systems and strategic access point placement solve these issues.

Newer construction in developments like Hennepin Village and along Eden Prairie Road offers pre-wiring opportunities, but smart home planning should happen before drywall goes up. Our installers work with local builders to ensure the right infrastructure is in place from day one.
