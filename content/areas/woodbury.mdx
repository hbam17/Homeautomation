While Woodbury's newer construction is generally automation-friendly, there are still challenges. Upgrading to Cat6 at strategic locations improves performance significantly.

Woodbury's rapid growth means some neighborhoods experience internet service variations. Homes on the city's eastern and southern edges may have limited fiber availability. A smart home installer can design systems that perform well on available bandwidth and recommend networking hardware that maximizes your connection.
