These additions sometimes create networking dead zones where the original construction meets later additions. ### Will smart home technology work with my older Burnsville home's electrical system?

Most Burnsville homes have adequate electrical systems for basic smart home upgrades like smart switches, thermostats, and security devices. Larger systems — especially those involving multiple amplifiers, powered speakers, and motorized shades — may require a panel upgrade from 100-amp to 200-amp service.
